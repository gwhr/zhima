# Specs: Free-tier Funnel and Support Contact

## Functional requirements

1. Free users can complete core trial path: topic selection, requirement confirmation, generation preview.
2. Full download actions must check recharge entitlement and provide clear upgrade prompt when blocked.
3. Workspace detail should render support contact section when enabled by platform config.
4. Support contact section must allow admin-configured title/description/QR URL.
5. Marketing homepage should reflect "毕设助手/辅导" positioning and compliant wording.

## Non-functional requirements

1. Funnel messaging should reduce compliance risk and avoid "代写" implication.
2. Download gating should not break preview capabilities.
3. UI should remain mobile/desktop readable for conversion-critical blocks.
